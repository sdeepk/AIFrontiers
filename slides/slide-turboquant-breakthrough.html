2.1 TurboQuant

TurboQuant: The Breakthrough

A two-stage KV cache compression algorithm. 6× compression. Zero accuracy loss.

Published March 25, 2026 by Google Research — within 24 hours it wiped $50 billion from memory chip stocks. The internet called it Pied Piper. To be formally presented at ICLR 2026. The KV cache is the inference memory that stores conversation context and grows with every token processed — this is the bottleneck TurboQuant targets. It achieves 3-bit quantization of the KV cache with zero downstream accuracy compromise, validated on LongBench, Needle-in-Haystack, and RULER benchmarks. No retraining required — applied to existing models.
