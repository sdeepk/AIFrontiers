Long-Term Plays: 6–24 Months

Use Case Why It Takes Time Payoff
Autonomous coding agents Codebase trust, evaluation rigour 2–3× dev velocity
Full customer journey orchestration Multi-system integration Major CSAT improvement
AI-driven compliance Domain complexity, regulatory approval Significant cost reduction
Predictive operations (AIOps) Historical data, trust calibration 20–30% less unplanned downtime
Knowledge graph enterprise AI Ontology design, graph population 5× AI analyst accuracy
These take longer because of trust calibration and data requirements, not technical difficulty. Autonomous coding agents need codebase understanding and evaluation rigour before you can trust them — the payoff of 2-3x dev velocity is on appropriate tasks, not all tasks. Full customer journey orchestration requires multi-system integration and edge case coverage that takes months to map. Knowledge graph-powered enterprise AI has the highest payoff at 5x AI analyst accuracy, but ontology design and graph population are substantial upfront investments. Emphasise that the payback period for all of these is measured in months, not years, once deployed.
