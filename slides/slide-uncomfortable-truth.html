1.3 The Evolving Role of Developers

The Uncomfortable Truth

The developer who only writes CRUD endpoints is being disrupted. The engineer who shapes systems, evaluates AI output, and thinks in orchestration is more valuable than ever.

Be direct here — this is meant to land hard. The developer who shapes systems, evaluates AI output, designs context, and thinks in orchestration is more valuable than ever AND increasingly rare. This is not about AI replacing developers. It is about which kind of developer becomes more valuable. The engineer who can design context architecture for an agent system is in a fundamentally different market position than the one who only writes CRUD endpoints.
