VMware Private AI Foundation

What: On-prem AI infrastructure for regulated enterprises — VMware vSphere + NVIDIA AI Enterprise
Why: Data sovereignty and compliance for BFSI, healthcare, defence
Signal: Sovereign AI, on-prem inference, air-gapped deployments are growing requirements
This directly addresses data sovereignty and compliance requirements of regulated Fortune 500 clients in BFSI, healthcare, and defence. Built on VMware vSphere plus NVIDIA AI Enterprise, it is on-premises AI infrastructure for enterprises that cannot put sensitive data in public cloud. Sovereign AI, on-prem inference, and air-gapped enterprise deployments are growing requirements — not a niche. This is especially relevant as TurboQuant and similar compression techniques make on-prem economically viable for more workloads.
