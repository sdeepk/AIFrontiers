The Expert Compression Loop

Encounter something you don't understand
Ask AI to explain at senior engineer level, with a concrete example in your stack
Deliberate mistake injection — Ask AI to generate a deliberately buggy version. Find the bug.
Explain what you learned back to AI — ask it to find gaps
Ask AI for the hardest interview question on this topic — answer it
Step 3 — the deliberate mistake injection — is the most underused and the most valuable. Ask AI to generate a deliberately buggy version of whatever you are learning, then find the bug yourself. This builds the debugging intuition that separates senior from junior engineers. Step 4 — explaining back to AI and asking it to find gaps — forces you to articulate your understanding, which is how deep learning happens. Step 5 — the hardest interview question — stress-tests your knowledge at the edge. Run this loop consistently and you will compress months of learning into weeks.
