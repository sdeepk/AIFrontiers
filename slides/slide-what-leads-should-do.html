This section is specifically for engineering leads and architects at Covalense. Individual developer habits are necessary but not sufficient — team-level enablement is what creates the compounding effect. These five actions are ordered by impact: standardising the toolkit removes the diversity tax, the context library creates shared IP, retros surface collective intelligence, measurement makes the case empirical, and the AI policy prevents compliance incidents. We will go through each one briefly. The key message: these are leadership responsibilities, not optional nice-to-haves.
