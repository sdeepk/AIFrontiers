What Actually Works: Building AI Culture

Champions program: 3–5 early adopters per team. Peer influence beats mandates.
Live demos with real data: Show AI on their actual codebase, not generic demos.
AI office hours: Weekly sessions to experiment safely and share discoveries.
Shared prompt libraries: Internal repo of prompts for your stack. This is team IP.
