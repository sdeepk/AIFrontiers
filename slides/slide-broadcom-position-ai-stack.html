Broadcom's Position in the AI Stack

1.5 Broadcom
Custom Silicon: Primary manufacturer for custom AI accelerators — Google TPUs, Meta MTIA, all Broadcom-fabbed
Scale: $69B VMware acquisition — one of the largest enterprise infrastructure platforms
Unique position: Intersection of AI hardware and AI software
Broadcom's $69B VMware acquisition in 2023, combined with CA Technologies, Symantec, and Brocade network fabric, makes it one of the largest enterprise infrastructure platforms globally. But the more strategically significant play is the hardware layer — Broadcom is the foundational silicon manufacturer for custom AI accelerators at hyperscale. Google's TPUs, Meta's MTIA chips, and emerging custom ASICs for Microsoft and ByteDance are all Broadcom-fabbed. This positions Broadcom uniquely at the intersection of AI hardware and AI software.
