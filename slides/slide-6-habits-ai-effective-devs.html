6 Habits of AI-Effective Developers

AI-First Drafting — with deliberate context engineering
Agentic RAG — not basic retrieval
Build Evaluation Frameworks First — before you ship
Spec-First Development — context-rich
Model-Appropriate Task Routing — right model, right task
Learning With AI — the expert compression loop

We'll go through each.

These six habits are the practical playbook for getting from Level 1 to Level 3. They are ordered intentionally: habits 1-2 are about context (the biggest lever), habits 3-4 are about quality (evaluation and specs), habit 5 is about efficiency (cost and speed), and habit 6 is about continuous growth. We will go through each one with concrete examples. The key message: these are daily habits, not occasional techniques.
