The Structural Shift

From

AI as a project. One-off experiments. Isolated POCs. No evaluation.

To

AI as an operating model. Embedded in workflows. Evaluated continuously. Scaled systematically.
This is the key takeaway from section 1.1. The gap between adoption and value is structural, not technical — per BCG AI Radar. Most companies have AI tools but few have AI workflows. The shift is from isolated experiments to embedded, continuously evaluated, systematically scaled AI. Transition: now let us talk about the technology that is making this real — AI agents.
