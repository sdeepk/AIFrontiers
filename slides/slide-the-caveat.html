The Caveat

Every category has one. AI gives you the first 70%. Humans own the outcome.

Pause here for emphasis. Every agent category we just covered — coding, customer service, documents, AIOps, BI — has a caveat. The pattern is consistent: AI gives you the first 70% of the work, but humans must own the outcome. Security review for code, human review for legal interpretation, kill switches for operations, schema quality for BI. This is not a limitation — it is the operating model.
