Walk through the spectrum left to right. Copilots just suggest — GitHub Copilot autocomplete. Workflow AI follows fixed steps — Zapier AI. Supervised agents have human-in-the-loop — Claude Code, Cursor, Salesforce Agentforce. Autonomous agents are self-directed — Devin, SWE-agent, Claude Opus 4.6 in agentic mode. Most enterprise production today sits in the supervised agent zone. The right edge is where the frontier is moving.
