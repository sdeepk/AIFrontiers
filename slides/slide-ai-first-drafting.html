Context Habits Habit 1

AI-First Drafting

Your first draft of anything — code, docs, test cases, PR descriptions — comes from AI. Your job is to direct, review, and refine.

The critical distinction: context engineering is the skill, not prompting. A prompt is what you say to the model. Context is everything the model knows — the system prompt, codebase context, retrieved documents, conversation history, examples, constraints, and output format. Optimising the context is the craft. Your first draft of code, docs, test cases, PR descriptions, and architecture proposals should come from AI. Your job shifts to directing, reviewing, and refining.
