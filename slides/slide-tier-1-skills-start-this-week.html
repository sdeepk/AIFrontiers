Emphasise: context engineering is not prompt engineering — it is architecture. It is the discipline of designing what documents, metadata, structure, and order go into the LLM's context window. Agentic RAG is not basic retrieve-and-generate — it involves routing, grading, self-correction, and hallucination checking. MCP integration is the dominant integration pattern of 2026. On evaluation: 70% of RAG systems have no systematic evaluation — this is a critical gap. AI-augmented SDLC means daily habit, not occasional use.
