The ROI Model

AI ROI = (Time saved × Cost of time) + (Error reduction × Cost of errors) + (Revenue uplift) − (Build + run + evaluation costs)

$150K–$500K
typical build cost
$500K–$3M
annual benefit
Use this formula in client conversations — it frames AI ROI in terms CFOs understand. The build cost range of $150K-$500K reflects that agentic systems cost more than simple pipelines. Annual run costs of $40K-$200K show that model costs are falling but agent orchestration costs are rising. Note that the formula includes evaluation and governance costs explicitly — these are often forgotten and cause budget surprises. The payback period of 6-18 months is for well-chosen, production-grade use cases. Quicker wins are possible with developer tooling — sometimes weeks, not months.
