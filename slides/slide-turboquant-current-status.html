TurboQuant: Current Status

Google open-source release: ~Q2 2026
Community ports: llama.cpp, MLX (Apple Silicon) in progress
Today's best approximation: Q4_K_M GGUF + Flash Attention 3
Note: 3-bit shows quality degradation in models under 8B parameters
For teams wanting to act now: complementary techniques like Flash Attention 3, speculative decoding, and Gemini's context caching already capture much of the practical efficiency gains available today. The 4-bit benchmark results hold strong; 3-bit degrades on models under 8B parameters so stick to 4-bit for smaller models. The practical recommendation is Q4_K_M GGUF quantization plus Flash Attention 3 as the best available stack until TurboQuant officially ships. Transition to fine-tuning: given how good these models are now, when do you actually need to fine-tune?
