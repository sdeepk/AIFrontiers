How TurboQuant Works

Stage 1: PolarQuant

Converts data vectors to polar coordinates after random rotation. Makes data distribution highly predictable. Eliminates per-block normalisation constants.

Stage 2: QJL

Quantized Johnson-Lindenstrauss. A 1-bit error correction layer that eliminates residual bias. Total error approaches the information-theoretic lower bound.
