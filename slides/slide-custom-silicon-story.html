The Custom Silicon Story

Why Custom Silicon

Hyperscalers designing own AI accelerators for cost and efficiency. Growing faster than GPU cloud.

Why Broadcom Wins

Primary beneficiary of the custom ASIC trend. Broadcom fabs the silicon that powers the largest AI infrastructure.
The custom ASIC business is growing faster than GPU cloud — hyperscalers are designing their own inference silicon for cost and efficiency reasons, and Broadcom is the primary beneficiary. This trend matters for IT services because it means inference costs will continue to fall structurally. As inference gets cheaper, more workloads become economically viable to automate with AI — the total addressable market for AI services grows. This is a Jevons Paradox dynamic.
