Questions I Get Asked Most

We have AI POCs everywhere but nothing in production. What do we do?
My team is resistant to AI tools. How do I change the culture?
Should I learn Python specifically for AI?
What does TurboQuant mean for the on-prem vs. cloud decision?
What's the fastest path to AI credibility without a big named reference?

Let's go deeper on any of these.

For the POCs question: production gap is always organisational — lack of evaluation, no LLMOps, no defined success metric. For team resistance: it is fear, not stubbornness — reframe AI as making individuals more valuable per hour. For Python: learn it minimally for scripting AI pipelines, but the skills that compound are context engineering and evaluation design. For TurboQuant: it shifts on-prem economics significantly — watch Q3 2026 when it lands in llama.cpp. For credibility: publish your methodology, contribute to open-source tooling, share case studies even when small.
