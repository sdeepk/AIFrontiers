Habit 6

Learning With AI

AI is the best personal tutor ever created. Use it for active learning, not passive answer retrieval.

AI is the best personal tutor ever created, but most people use it for passive answer retrieval — asking a question and copying the answer. The real value is in active learning. The expert compression loop on the next slide is extraordinarily efficient: a developer running it consistently accelerates skill acquisition by an order of magnitude compared to documentation reading alone. The key insight is that you can learn from AI in ways that are impossible with static resources — it adapts to your level, your stack, and your gaps in real time.
