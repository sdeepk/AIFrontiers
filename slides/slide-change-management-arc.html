The Change Management Arc

Awareness
Town halls, demos, leadership messaging
Engagement
Champions program, office hours, safe experimentation
Adoption
Standardised tools, shared prompts, daily use
Advocacy
Internal case studies, measured results, peer teaching
This arc is sequential — you cannot skip stages. Most organisations try to jump from Awareness straight to Adoption and wonder why it fails. The Engagement phase is where the champions program and office hours live — this is where people develop personal conviction. Adoption requires standardised tools and daily use, not just occasional experimentation. Advocacy is the compounding stage: internal case studies with measured results create peer teaching loops that sustain momentum without management pushing.
