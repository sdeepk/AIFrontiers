Tier 3 is the Game

75% of Fortune 500 need a partner, not a vendor. This is the IT services opportunity.

IDC forecasts $632 billion in global AI spend by 2028 — the vast majority flows through implementation partners. These Tier 3 companies are not building AI labs; they are looking for partners who understand their compliance, their data, and their workflows. This is the addressable market for IT services. The differentiator is not technology — it is trust, delivery speed, and domain expertise.
