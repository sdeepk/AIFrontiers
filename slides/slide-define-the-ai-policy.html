Define the AI Policy

Action 5
What goes to external AI APIs. What does not.
Customer PII, unreleased product details, credentials, regulatory data — NEVER to external APIs
Absence of a policy is a policy of 'anything goes'
This should be a documented, signed policy — not individual developer judgment. Customer PII, unreleased product details, authentication credentials, and regulatory data should never go to external AI APIs. The absence of a policy is effectively a policy of "anything goes," which eventually creates a compliance incident. Define this before you need it, not after an incident. This is especially important given the India DPDP Act implications for data used in AI inference and the EU AI Act enforcement escalating through 2026.
