AI Evaluation as a Service

Strategic Priority 5
A practice line, not just an internal tool
Offer: We will audit your AI system and tell you if it actually works
Natural extension of quality engineering heritage
Creates advisory relationship → converts to implementation
Position this as an independent service line: "We will audit your AI system and tell you whether it is actually working." This requires three capabilities: evaluation methodology (LLM-as-judge, task-specific metrics, factual consistency, adversarial testing), red-teaming capability (prompt injection testing, bias detection, edge case mapping), and EU AI Act compliance assessment (risk classification, human oversight audit, documentation review). This is a natural extension of quality engineering heritage and creates an advisory relationship that converts to implementation engagements. The land-and-expand motion is powerful here.
