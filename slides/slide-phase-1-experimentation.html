Phase 1: Experimentation

What: Integrating LLMs into internal tooling, exploring GitHub Copilot
What worked: Developer velocity gains were immediate — measurable improvement in boilerplate and PR turnaround
What failed: "AI-first" projects with no defined success metrics died in committee
Key learning: "The technical risk is lower than you think. The organisational risk is higher."
The key learning: the technical risk is lower than you think, but the organisational risk is higher. Projects without defined success metrics died in committee, not because the technology failed. Developer velocity gains with Copilot were immediate and measurable — boilerplate reduction and faster PR turnaround. The lesson: start with something that has clear, agreed metrics before you write a line of code.
