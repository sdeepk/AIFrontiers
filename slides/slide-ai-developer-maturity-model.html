The AI-Assisted Developer Maturity Model

Part 4: Developer Enablement
Level 0 AI-unaware No AI tools in workflow
Level 1 AI-aware Autocomplete and search
Level 2 AI-fluent AI across full SDLC with evaluation
Level 3 AI-collaborative Multi-agent systems, context architecture
Level 4 AI-native Builds and evaluates AI systems for others

Where are you? Where do you want to be?

Ask the room: where do you honestly sit today? Most developers are between Level 0 and Level 1. The jump from Level 1 to Level 2 is about integrating AI across the full SDLC with evaluation — not just autocomplete. Level 3 is the force multiplier: these developers design multi-agent systems and context architecture that uplift the whole team. Level 4 is architect-level — building and evaluating AI systems for others. The next few slides define the concrete target for Covalense.
