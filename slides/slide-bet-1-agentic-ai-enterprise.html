Bet 1: Agentic AI at Enterprise Scale

Why: The shift from AI-as-tool to AI-as-colleague is the defining platform shift of this decade
Invest in: Agent reliability engineering, multi-agent orchestration, A2A + MCP integration
Timeline: 2026 = production-grade for early movers. 2027–2028 = mainstream.
This is the biggest bet of the five. Multi-agent systems that can operate autonomously for hours are already in production — Claude Opus 4.6 agentic mode and OpenAI's Deep Research are doing knowledge work at scale. The investment areas to emphasise: agent reliability engineering is the biggest white space, because most agents that exist today are fragile. Also invest in agentic evaluation frameworks — you cannot deploy what you cannot measure. The specific skills to build are A2A + MCP integration expertise, which we covered in Part 1.
