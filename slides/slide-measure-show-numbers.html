Track before and after across these four metrics. Story points per sprint per developer shows throughput. PR cycle time from open to merge shows velocity. Time to first commit for new team members shows onboarding speed — AI plus spec-driven onboarding can dramatically reduce ramp-up time. Bug escape rate shows whether AI-assisted code reviews are catching more issues pre-merge. When the numbers go up, your case for further AI investment is empirical, not anecdotal. Show these numbers to leadership monthly.
