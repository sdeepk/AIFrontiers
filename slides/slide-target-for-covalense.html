The Target for Covalense

Every developer: Level 2 minimum
Senior developers: Level 3
Leads and architects: Level 4
This is not aspirational — this is the 2026 target. Level 2 minimum means every developer uses AI across the full SDLC with evaluation, not just autocomplete. Senior devs at Level 3 means they can design context architecture and orchestrate agents for the team. Leads at Level 4 means they can build, evaluate, and assure AI systems for clients.
